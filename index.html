Ariana Grande

Biography

American singer-songwriter-actress Ariana Grande was born in Boca Raton, Florida, on June 26, 1993. At the age of fifteen, she made her stage debut in Broadway's "13". Grande became well-known for her portrayal as Cat Valentine on Nickelodeon's "Sam & Cat" and "Victorious" series. With the release of her debut album "Yours Truly" in 2013—which included the breakthrough tune "The Way"—her musical career took off. Her position as a pop superstar was cemented by later albums such as "My Everything," "Dangerous Woman," and "Thank U, Next." With a wide vocal range, she has created several singles that have reached the top of the charts.
